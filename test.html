Welcome to TUM

Technological University (Mandalay) is a proud leader in Myanmar’s engineering education. Located in Pathein Gyi, Mandalay, we are committed to shaping future innovators through advanced laboratories, skilled faculty, and cutting-edge programs that meet industry demands.

About Technological University (Mandalay)

Technological University (Mandalay) is one of Myanmar’s oldest engineering universities, spanning 65.37 acres. We are committed to excellence in teaching, innovation, and student development.
