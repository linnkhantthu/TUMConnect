History

Civil engineering program has been formed since the beginning of the Government Technical Institute at 1955. Every Head of Civil Engineering tried very hard for our department to promote the technological field and therefore, nowadays we are proud of our department. And the students who had been studied from the civil engineering department are well done for job opportunities to regional field.

Vision

Civil Engineering Department of the Technological University (Mandalay) is envisaged to become a department which trains students systematically to quality for the workplace requirements and to grow into outstanding Civil Engineers.

Mission

  • To achieve effective and comprehensive teaching and learning experiences by utilizing modern teaching aids
  • To implement the capacity building for teaching staffs and laboratory assistants
  • To identify workplace qualifications and to evaluate curriculum continually

Objectives

  • To increase the pass percentage of undergraduate Civil Engineering students to more than 85%
  • To receive complaints from customers no more than 5%

Degree Awards

  • Bachelor of Engineering B.E (Civil)
  • Master of Engineering M.E (Civil)

Research Areas

  • Construction materials research
  • Water resources engineering research
  • Geotechnical engineering research
  • Transportation engineering research
  • Latest Projects(Fourth Year Project Title 2024-2025 Academic Year):
    • Modelling and Design of Nirvana Villa
    • Modelling and Design of Four-Storey RC Building with Seismic Isolators
    • Modelling the Construction and Design of the Mandalay - Pyin Oo Lwin Highway
    • Model Construction of Swing Bridge
